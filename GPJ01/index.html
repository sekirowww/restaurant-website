ABOUT US

Our restaurant is well known for having some of the best steaks in Hong Kong. If you're looking to impress your guests with an unfor-gettable meal, then look no further than Lion Rock. You won't find another place like it. It's not just amazing food but also an incredible experience.

Our passions

We truly believe that the best moments in life are those spent with loved ones, sharing food, friendship, laughter and em-bracing the joy of the Australian table. Join us in one of our restaurants for a magical time with your friends and family, forgetting those day to day distractions. It's the perfect time to talk, laugh, eat, drink and be together!

Fantastic food

Our love affair with food is long-term. We're passionate about making good food and memorable times since 1988. When it comes to ingredients, we never compromise. Only the best goes into creating each hearty dish served to your table. It's not just the food that's fresh. We're constantly innovating our recipes, improving our service and sprucing up our decor to provide the best dining experience for you.

Social responsibility

Those of us here at steak house have meticulously reviewed our carbon footprint and have crafted a strategy that aims to meet those wider net zero targets. We are making changes behind the scenes, every single day. This includes sustainable choices, tracking potential improvements, focusing on reducing food waste and zero waste.
